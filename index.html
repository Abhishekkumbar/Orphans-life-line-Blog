We Care For Your Children

1. Disabilities are not always visible

You play a key role in monitoring your child's development and identifying any signs of delays or challenges.

2. Early intervention is important

The first three years of a child’s development are the most crucial. Intervention at this stage before any formal diagnosis is essential.

3. You are not alone

52 million children in India have symptoms of developmental disabilities, and there is a wealth of resources you can leverage for support.

General Orphan Support

We are providing following Support to sponsored Orphans

  • These donations contribute towards helping more than one orphan. Your donations are used to provide for the orphans’ education, books, stationary items and food in some instances. General Orphans Support can make a significant difference in an orphan’s life.
  • Allows you to support a specific child on an ongoing basis. Your donation, either through monthly or annual payment is given directly to the orphan’s family or guardian. You can immediately view your orphans complete profile including Educational and Medical Reports, Online.

    Benefits for Orphans

    We take care of following basic needs of the sponsored orphans.

    • Subsistence(monthly ration, clothes, hygiene kits);
    • Education (includes school fee, books, school supplies and two uniforms);
    • Health (includes medical checkups, immunization);
    • Social upliftment (includes Extra curricular activities)
